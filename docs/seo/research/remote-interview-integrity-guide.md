## Legal / consent
- EEOC: "While the technology may be evolving, anti-discrimination laws still apply." AI selection tools can "mask and perpetuate bias." Employers remain liable under Title VII for disparate impact even from vendor tools; a human-in-the-loop is recommended.

## SERP / competitor angle
Who ranks for "remote interview integrity" and adjacent: Fabric, Karat, HireVue, Incruiter, The Interview Guys, HootRecruit, withSherlock. Most posts are either (a) alarmist "candidates are cheating" listicles, or (b) vendor proctoring pitches focused on timed tests / lockdown browsers.

**Gap to fill:** A calm, structured pillar that (1) defines interview integrity for LIVE interviews (not timed tests), (2) gives an operational framework (deter → detect → decide) with a human in the loop, (3) takes fairness and false positives seriously, and (4) tells buyers how to choose tooling without lockdown/surveillance overreach. Nobody ranking today balances the threat data with candidate-fairness and false-positive warnings.
