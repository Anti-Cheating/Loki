New domain (0 authority). Target long-tail, high-intent, low-competition queries first.
Build 3 topic clusters; each cluster has one pillar page + supporting articles that
interlink. Every article links to `/demo` and at least one `/features` anchor.

## Article brief template (use for every entry above)
- **Target query:** <primary phrase>
- **Search intent:** <informational | commercial | transactional>
- **Working title (H1):** <includes primary phrase naturally>
- **Slug:** <kebab-case>
- **Word count:** 1500+ (pillar 2500+)
- **Must include:** 2+ cited external stats, an FAQ block (feeds FAQPage schema),
  internal links to /demo + one /features anchor, one comparison mention.
- **Meta description:** <=155 chars, includes primary phrase.

## Cadence
2 new articles/month + optimize 1 existing article/month. Re-check Search Console
queries monthly and promote any impression-getting phrase to its own page.
