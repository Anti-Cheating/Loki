Why lockdown tools fall short

They guard the wrong door

Locking a browser stops a candidate from opening a tab. It does nothing about a phone propped behind the laptop, an AI assistant on a second device, or an answer read from a printed sheet. The cheating moved. The tools did not.

Why Trueyy fits hiring

It reads intent, not just motion

Trueyy was made for the conversation, not the quiz. It looks at how an answer is built and where attention goes, then gives your interviewer a read they can actually use while the call is still live.

If you run timed certification exams at scale, classic proctoring still has its place. If you run live interviews and want to know whether the person did the thinking, that is exactly what Trueyy is for.
